Click "Prev" or "Next" (or use your arrow keys) to cycle through cohorts. Click the link at the top of the page to switch between new freshmen and transfer student cohorts.

Each chart contains all students from that cohort who were ever College of Environmental Design majors, as of end-of-term snapshots. The "Multiple Majors" category contains students who are both a CED and some other major. The "Other Major" category contains students who only (as of that term) have majors outside of CED.
